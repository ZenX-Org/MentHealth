Volunteer to man the helpline:

  • At MentHealth we offer support from professionally trained and skilled volunteers.
  • The volunteers are selected with great care, chosen for their ability to listen with empathy and understanding.
  • Each volunteer has to undergo training for a minimum of six months before they can start dealing with callers.

Volunteer for publicity & fundraising:

  • Help us spread awareness!
  • Students can talk about and represent MentHealth in their schools and colleges.
  • Bloggers can blog about us in their community, link us or help advertise MentHealth by giving us a small space in their blog or website.
  • Alternatively, a volunteer can use their Twitter/Facebook or any other social networking platform to promote MentHealth among friends and family.
  • A volunteer can direct potential donors to MentHealth.
  • As for Corporates, we would be thrilled if you can adopt MentHealth as a part of your "Corporate Social Responsibility".

How does your DONATION help?

    MentHealth volunteers and beneficiaries are grateful for your donations.
  • Your donations help us broad base the fight against suicide by aiding us in developing a larger volunteer force:
  • To provide 24x7 suicide prevention emotional support hotline services.
  • Increase the awareness campaign.
  • Fight against the debilitating effects of stigma associated with depression, suicide and mental illness.
  • Support suicide survivors to cope with their grief and loss.
  • Develop strategies to reach out to a larger group of people to prevent suicides.
  • Conduct research, educate and train peer support gatekeepers to become first response interventionists in order to pre-empt suicide.
